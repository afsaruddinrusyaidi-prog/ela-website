Track Spotlight · SLDP

Where the Journey Begins

SLDP anchors a fully funded, five-year residential leadership incubator in exclusive partnership with Kolej Yayasan Saad (KYS), Melaka — transforming top secondary school students into the premier foundation layer of our ASEAN network.

👤 Ages 13–17 💰 Fully Funded by Axiata Foundation ⚖️ Equity-First Selection
  • ✦
    The 3Cs FrameworkElite foundations in Communication, Critical Thinking and Creativity — delivered through boot camps, experiential modules and personal mentoring.
  • ✦
    Experiential GrowthStudents are pushed past comfort zones via real-world community impact projects, business simulations and structured self-reflection blocks.
  • ✦
    Full Scholarship Scope100% of financial liabilities covered across the five-year loop — tuition, textbooks, uniforms, a laptop and all dedicated SLDP boot camp costs.
  • ✦
    Socioeconomic MissionEntry is fiercely competitive, yet the Axiata Foundation intentionally prioritises outstanding candidates from underprivileged, suburban and low-income backgrounds to drive equitable upward mobility.

The Selection Pipeline

Initial Online Application

Academic screening straight after the primary school cycle — top-tier marks plus an outstanding extracurricular leadership portfolio.

The Entrance Examination

Rigorous central aptitude testing measuring analytical, verbal and logical problem-solving baselines.

Residence-Based Selection — “Ax-tivity”

An intense, multi-day residential camp at KYS: team challenges, presentation sprints and behavioural interviews that isolate raw leadership character.
