SLDP

School Leadership Development Programme

Our journey begins at the grassroots level. SLDP anchors a fully funded, five-year residential leadership incubator in exclusive partnership with Kolej Yayasan Saad (KYS), Melaka — transforming top secondary school students into the premier foundation layer of our ASEAN network.

Ages 13–17 Fully Funded by Axiata Foundation Equity-First Selection

  • ✦

The Selection Pipeline

A Multi-Stage Filtration Framework

Initial Online Application

Academic screening straight after the primary school cycle — requiring top-tier marks combined with an outstanding extracurricular leadership portfolio.

The Entrance Examination

Shortlisted candidates undergo rigorous central aptitude testing measuring analytical, verbal and logical problem-solving baselines.

Residence-Based Selection — “Ax-tivity”

An intense, multi-day residential selection camp at KYS: team challenges, presentation sprints and behavioural interviews that isolate raw leadership character.
