About the Pipeline

The Axiata Young Talent Programme develops leaders at four life stages — school, university, digital careers and the executive floor. No single track is the story. The pipeline is.

01School

SLDP

School Leadership Development Programme
Secondary · Ages 13–175-Year ResidencyFully Funded

Leadership foundations built during secondary school — a residential incubator in partnership with Kolej Yayasan Saad, Melaka.

  • Full scholarship: tuition, boarding, books and boot camps
  • Equity-first selection prioritising underserved talent
  • Multi-stage entry capped by the “Ax-tivity” residential camp
02University

ULDP

University Leadership Development Programme
Undergraduate15+ Years RunningNational Cohorts

Malaysia's most promising undergraduates, sharpened through the AYTP leadership curriculum for over fifteen years.

  • Structured leadership modules and bootcamp intensives
  • Industry exposure with Axiata leaders and partners
  • A cohort network that carries into every career
03Digital

ADLP

Axiata Digital Leaders Programme
Early CareerDigital-FirstASEAN Scope

Digital-first leadership for the technology frontier — where fluency in tech meets enterprise judgment.

  • Built for ASEAN's most demanding digital arenas
  • Technology fluency paired with commercial acumen
  • The bridge between early career and enterprise leadership

The Shared DNA

Whatever the entry point, every track builds on the same AYTP development core.

The 3Cs Framework

Elite foundations in Communication, Critical Thinking and Creativity — delivered through boot camps, experiential modules and personal mentoring.

Experiential Growth

Real-world community impact projects, business simulations and structured self-reflection — growth past the comfort zone, at every stage.

Backed With Purpose

Axiata Foundation backing with an equity-first mission — intentionally opening doors for outstanding talent from every background.
