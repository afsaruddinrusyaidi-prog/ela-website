The AYTP Pipeline

Four Tracks.
One Continuity Engine.

ELA sustains the alumni journey across every Axiata Young Talent Programme track — a leadership pipeline running from secondary school residencies to CEO-grade development.

SLDP

School Leadership Development Programme
Secondary · KYS Melaka

A fully funded five-year residential leadership incubator in partnership with Kolej Yayasan Saad — the grassroots entry point of the entire pipeline.

ULDP

University Leadership Development Programme
Undergraduate

Sharpening high-potential university talent with structured leadership modules, real-world exposure and the AYTP capability framework.

ADLP

Axiata Digital Leaders Programme
15+ Years Running

Over fifteen years of shaping digital-first leaders equipped for the technology frontier of ASEAN's corporate landscape.

YCDP

Young CEO Development Programme
12+ Years Running

Twelve-plus years of grooming young executives on the accelerated path to C-suite readiness and enterprise leadership.

✦ Our ecosystem also embraces the Axiata All-Star Bestari Scholars — extending the network beyond the four core tracks.
